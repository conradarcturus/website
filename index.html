Biography

PhD Student
University of Washington
Computer Science and Engineering
anied (at) cs.washington.edu

I use my background in cognitive science to analyze and improve human-human interaction over technology. I've just completed my year at UW. I'm advised by Sean Munson at Human Centered Design and Engineering. I work with the CSE HCI Lab and the Prosocial Computing Lab. I'm also a member of DUB.

Previously, I worked as Clinical Research Coordinator at Massachusetts General Hospital in the Neuropsychology Laboratory and earned my bachelors degree in Computer Science with a minor in Anthropology at Boston University.
